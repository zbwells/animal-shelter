Get To Know Us

WCAS serves Wise County, Virginia. We are a small shelter located in Norton, Virginia. We accept donations of food, cleaning products, and more.

If you want to know which items/resources we currently need, please contact us.

We adopt dogs out directly, but also work with local rescues to foster the animals that come through our doors. For information about fostering or adopting, give us a call.

Our kennels are usually full, so we encourage all potential adoptees to stop by and see the pets.
